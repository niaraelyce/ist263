THE POLICY

The Ultimate Guide to Booking!

My site requires that all clients MUST make a deposit of $15 in order to complete the booking request. This will go toward your total amount due at the end of your service. Please note that your appointment will not be confirmed if this deposit is not sent within 24-hours of the request for an appointment.


Deposits are due 24-hours after booking. Deposits can be sent via Apple Pay, Cashapp or Venmo to this number: (123-456-7890).


Please arrive to your appointment on the scheduled date, at the scheduled time. There is a 15-minute grace period. After 15 minutes, your appointment will be canceled, and deposit forfeited. There is a maximum 48-hour cancellation/reschedule notice that is required. Please note that if you are cancelling the day of, or any time after the 48-hour period, your deposit will be forfeited. No call, no show clients are not permitted to book with us again until a discussion is held as to why this occurred.


Apple Pay, Cash, Cashapp & Venmo are all acceptable payment methods for your final total. I do work from my home, so upon receiving your booking confirmation you will also receive a protected link where my address is listed for you to follow.


Please do not hesitate to reach out with any questions!
